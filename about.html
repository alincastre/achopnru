ℹ️ About Patient Navigation & Referral Unit (PNRU)
📌 Mandate & Core Functions

Our Mission

To provide seamless, timely, and efficient health navigation and referral assistance to patients, ensuring continuous access to primary, secondary, and tertiary healthcare facilities across the health network.

Our Vision

Effective and efficient means of managing the client’s transitions through medical and social services. Clients are assigned to a Patient Navigator to assist them in entering medical care, clarifying medical concerns, and providing referrals.

Scope of Services

  • 24/7 Inter-facility referral coordination
  • Emergency transport dispatch and guidance
  • Outpatient navigation and diagnostic scheduling
📌 Purpose & Services

Purpose

To implement an effective patient navigation and referral system. This system aims to enhance patient care coordination, streamline referral processes, and improve health outcomes for the residents of Antipolo City.

Services

Patient Transfer Assistance: Assist patients requiring transfer between hospitals or home-to-hospital transfers.

DOH Calabarzon Coordination: Coordinating with DOH Calabarzon PNRS for specialized treatment, emergency care, or regional follow-up care.

Ambulance Services: Offering emergency transport for patients requiring urgent medical attention.

Inquiries & Helpdesk: Answering questions and queries regarding various services of the City Health Office.

📌 Patient Navigation and Referral Unit Workflow
  1. Entry Point: Patient arrives via Outpatient Department (OPD), Emergency Room (ER), or is referred from an external primary care clinic.
  2. Screening & Intake: Navigator assesses clinical needs, insurance status, and social barriers (transportation, language, etc.).
  3. Internal / External Triage:
    • Internal: Directing the patient to a specialist within the same facility network.
    • External: Coordinating a transfer to a higher-level facility or a specialized diagnostic center.
  4. Coordination: PNRU staff handles administrative needs—booking appointments, transmitting medical records, arranging transportation, and securing prior authorizations.
  5. Follow-Up: Ensures the patient completes referrals, receives results, and maintains ongoing care support.
